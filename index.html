A — Buzz / FizzBuzz
3+ players · Verbal · Any drink
Count around the circle. Replace multiples of 7 with "Buzz". In FizzBuzz: multiples of 3 = Fizz, multiples of 5 = Buzz, multiples of 15 = FizzBuzz. Say the wrong thing or hesitate? Drink and restart from 1.
B — Rhyme Time
3+ players · Verbal · Any drink
One person says a word. Go around the circle — each person must say a word that rhymes. No repeats. Hesitate, fail to rhyme, or repeat = drink and the next person picks a new word.
C — 21
3+ players · Verbal · Any drink
Count to 21 around the circle. You can say 1, 2, or 3 numbers per turn. Whoever says 21 must drink AND sets a new rule (e.g. say 7 as "banana", reverse direction at 11). Rules stack every round.
D — Snap Drinking
2–6 players · Needs cards · Any drink
Play classic snap with a standard deck. When someone shouts "Snap!" on a matching pair — last person to slap the pile drinks. False snap (wrong cards)? You drink instead.
E — Staring Contest
2 players · Physical · Shot recommended
Two players lock eyes. No blinking, no laughing, no looking away. First to break drinks. Everyone else can do anything to make them break — except touch them.
F — Speed Round
4+ players · Verbal · Any drink
One person is the host. Rapid-fire questions at the group. First to shout the correct answer gives out a drink. Wrong answer = you drink. Host rotates every 5 questions.
G — Liar's Dice (吹牛 Chui Niu)
2–6 players · Needs dice cups · SG favourite
Each player has 5 dice hidden under a cup. Players bid on how many of a certain number exist across ALL cups (e.g. "four 3s"). Next player must bid higher or call the bluff. If you call and the bidder is wrong, they drink. If the bid was correct, you drink. Last player with dice wins.
H — Gong Show / Pass the Flower
4+ players · Needs music · Party classic
One person controls the music (eyes closed, back turned). Pass any object around the circle while the music plays. When the music stops, whoever is holding the object drinks. The music controller rotates each round.
I — 5-10 (五十 / 打手指) 🇸🇬
2 players · Physical · SG classic · First to win 2 in a row
Setup: Do Scissors Paper Stone to decide who shouts first each round.

How to play: Both players simultaneously show 0, 5, or 10 fingers. The shouter calls out a number — 0, 5, 10, 15, or 20 — predicting the combined total of both hands. Then the other player shouts their prediction. Only one call per player per round.

Winning a round: The player whose call matches the actual combined total wins that round. Loser drinks. If neither or both match, no drink — replay.

Hand rule: You cannot show the same number twice in a row. If you showed 0 (both fists) last round, you must change your hand next round. Caught repeating = automatic loss of that round.

Winning the game: You must win 2 rounds in a row to win. One win resets if your opponent wins the next round. The first player to string back-to-back wins makes the other person drink a full penalty shot or nominated amount.
K — Finger Guessing (猜手指)
2 players · Physical · SG classic
Both players simultaneously show 0–5 fingers with one hand while shouting a number 0–10. The player who correctly guesses the combined total of both hands wins. Loser drinks. If both guess correctly or both are wrong, replay. Play to 3 wins.
